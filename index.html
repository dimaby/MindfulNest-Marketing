MindfulNest
Order in Property Search

Today, all buyer-agent communication happens in WhatsApp — and after a few properties, the chat becomes chaos: links, photos, notes from viewings, everything mixed up.

MindfulNest is a structured workspace where buyers and agents interact within separate "cases" for each property. Below is the product concept and a survey for expert validation. Your assessment as a practitioner is invaluable to us.

⏱️ 10–15 minutes 📋 14 questions 🔒 Anonymous if preferred 🎯 For real estate agents
Part 1 · Context

Why buying property turns into "WhatsApp chaos"

We conducted a series of interviews with buyers in Limassol, Paphos, and Nicosia. The pattern repeats — the bigger the budget and choice, the louder the pain.

WhatsApp approach today

🌀 How it looks now

  • × Links, photos, voice messages and notes mixed in one stream
  • × Impossible to quickly find a property after 2 weeks
  • × Clients ask agents to resend the same info 3 times
  • × Comparing 5 properties — only manually, in Excel or in your head
  • × Notes get lost or don't arrive during property viewings
  • × After making a decision — history is lost forever
MindfulNest approach

✨ How it works with us

  • ✓ Each property is a separate "case" with photos, documents, chat and notes
  • ✓ AI structures voice notes into pros / cons / highlights
  • ✓ Compare properties by key parameters in 1 tap
  • ✓ Shared chat within the case — buyer and agent see the same thing
  • ✓ "On-site" mode during viewings: photos, video, voice — all linked to property and location
  • ✓ AI insights: what really matters for this buyer
Part 2 · Solution

What is MindfulNest — screen by screen

Mobile app (iOS/Android). Below are key screens from the buyer's perspective. Agent's screen is mirrored.

Main ScreenCases

Each property is a separate case, not a message in a feed

Instead of endless messaging, the buyer sees property cards: photo, price, agent, status (New / Favorite / Viewed / Archive). History for each property is stored within the case and never gets lost.

  • Filters and statuses: separate track for each property
  • Case "tail" — days in progress, last update
  • Instant search across all cases
Property CaseAI Pros/Cons

All property information — on one screen

Inside the case: photos, specs, notes, documents, chat with agent. AI automatically highlights pros and cons from notes and voice messages — the buyer doesn't drown in data, but makes decisions.

  • "Pros & Cons" block is compiled by AI from notes after viewing
  • Property rating 1–5 stars right from the card
  • Add to comparison — with one tap
On-site ModeGPSVoice → AI

"On-site" mode: nothing gets lost at the property

During a property viewing, the buyer activates On-site Mode. Photos, videos, voice notes and text are automatically linked to this property and its geolocation. AI immediately breaks down voice into pros / cons / highlights.

  • GPS confirmation — recording is actually from the property
  • Voice Note → structured text in seconds
  • "Recent Captures" timeline with AI processing
ComparisonAI Insight

Property comparison — and AI verdict

The buyer selects 2–5 properties and sees a table: price, m², €/m², distance to sea, rating. AI writes a brief conclusion: which property best fits this specific client's priorities.

  • Automatic highlighting of best value in each column
  • AI Insight considers notes and ratings, not just numbers
  • "View Winner" — quick return to the favorite
In-case ChatAI Summary

Shared case chat — without chaos

Chat lives inside the case, not in a general feed. Files, agreements, viewing dates — everything is linked to a specific property. AI Summary condenses long discussions into 2–3 lines.

  • Attachments automatically go to case "Docs"
  • AI Summary: "Second viewing scheduled for Saturday 11:00"
  • Chat history is not lost when changing phones

What's new in MindfulNest compared to WhatsApp/CRM

We will validate these differences with the survey — this is where we need expert assessment from agents.

🧠

AI works with voice, not CRM fields

Agent and buyer don't need to fill anything out. Voice note on-site → AI automatically breaks it down into pros, cons and highlights and attaches to the case.

🗂️

Cases instead of chats

The unit of work is the property, not the conversation. Files, chat, notes, documents and comparison live inside the case, not scattered across messengers.

📍

On-site mode with GPS

Recording linked to property geolocation — so buyer and agent always see where photos and voice came from, even a month later.

⚖️

AI comparison by priorities

Not just a table — AI considers buyer's ratings and notes, and suggests: "Property #1 is better for you in terms of price / view / area ratio".

💬

Shared context for buyer and agent

Case sharing in one tap. Agent sees exactly what the buyer sees — no forwarding, copy-paste or "send it again".

📅

Viewing schedule + documents

Viewing calendar and documents — not separate Google files, but part of the case. After the deal, everything stays in history.

API Connector · Integrations

MindfulNest integrates into your existing systems, not replaces them

We understand — your agency already has a CRM, property portal, calendar and messengers. MindfulNest doesn't ask you to migrate. Open REST + Webhooks API connects cases to your systems in hours, not weeks.

One API — to unite messenger, CRM and property portal

Properties from your CRM are automatically created as cases in MindfulNest. Notes, photos and AI insights from viewings — return back to the client card in CRM. Chat and documents sync both ways. Buyer doesn't change habits, agency doesn't lose data.
